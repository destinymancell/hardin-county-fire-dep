Firefighter Support Fund

The Savannah and Hardin County Firefighters Support Fund was established to help our firefighters and the families in their time of need. If an active firefighter or a member of their immediate family is injured or hospitialized and requires travel or time lost from work for them or their dependants the Fund will donate up to $200.00.

Immediate family includes spouse and thieir children below the age of 18. If a Department or District would like to organize a benifit for an individual in their department the fund will donate up to $100.00 to help with startup costs. Any additional or special requests for funds will be discussed and voted on by the Firefighter's Support Fund.

How To Obtain A Support Fund Paper

To Attain a FireFighter Support Fund Paper you will need to go to the City Fire Department. It is located at 465 Main Street, Savannah, TN 38372.
