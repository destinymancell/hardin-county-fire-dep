Application Process

1. Complete the application with a copy of driver's license and beneficiary form. Turn it in to what will be your district chief or the department chief on duty, located at the courthouse.

2. Your applications will have to be signed by both for approval of being on the department.

3. You must meet with your district cheif and fire department chief in order to be voted on to that specific district. This will be the final application process.

4. Complete the required tarining within the first 2 years after acceptance on the department.

5. Complete a 6 month probationary period after you have completed you New Recruit Class. Until you have completed your New Recruit Class you are Not allowed to respond. After your New Recruit Class you are not allowed to drive any fire department owned equipment until you have completed you Emergency Vehicle Driving Course

6. You can not obtain a firefighter tag until your probitionary period is up.

Requirements to join

1. Must be 18 years of age.

2. Must live in Hardin County.

3. MUST have a valid state of TN drivers license. You MUST provide a copy of driver's license with the application.

4. Subject to a driver's license and background check.

5. No major health conditions.

6. Must have not be convicted of any felonies and/or misdemeanors.

7. Must be approved by the distrcit, district chief, and fire chief.

8. Must have high moral standards, and must take all required classes.
