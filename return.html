About The Fire Fighters

Fighters Prayer: When I am called to duty, God, wherever flames may rage, give me strength to save a life, whatever be its age. Help me embrace a little child before it is too late, or save an older person from the horror of that fate. Enable me to be alert, and hear the weakest shout, quickly and efficiently to put the fire out. I want to fill my calling, to give the best in me, to guard my friend and neighbor, and protect his property. And if according to Your will I must answer death's call, bless with your protecting hand, my family one and all

"Hardin County Fire Department is a combination fire department with 4 full time paid personnel and approximately 190 volunteers. We protect all of Hardin County Tennessee outside the city limits of Savannah and Saltillo, we operate out of 22 fire stations with 85 pieces of fire apparatus and 10 boats."

The Mission Statement: "To ensure the safety and welfare of the citizens of Hardin County through mitigation, preparing for,responding to, and recovering from any natural or human cause hazard, emergency, or disaster."

Melvin Martin is the Hardin County Fire Cheif and Emergency Managment Director. Marilyn White is the Hardin County Managment Deputy Director. Danny Franks is the Hardin County Fire Administrative Assistant. Jessica Beckham is the Hardin County EMA/Fire Administrative Assistant.

Location

"Hardin County is located in southwest Tennessee 95 miles east of Memphis, with a population of 26,000 and 578 square miles. We provide protection to Shiloh National Military Park and 50 miles of the Tennessee River, as well as Pickwick Hydro Electric Dam and Pickwick Lake. We responded to more than 1,000 calls a year."
